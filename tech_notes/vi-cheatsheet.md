# Vi Cheatsheet

----------------------

| Command |     Meaning     |
|:-------:|-----------------|
|h        |move cursor left |
|j        |move cursor down |
|k        |move cursor up   |
|l        |move cursor right|
|a        |add after char   |
|i        |add before char  |
|A        |add end of line  |
|I        |add start of line|
|o        |add below line   |
|O        |add above line   |
|dd       |delete one line  |
|x        |delete one char  |
|:w       |save             |
|:q       |quit             |
|:wq      |save and quit    |
|:w!      |force save       |
|:q!      |force quit       |
